B2B IT Services

Scaling Organic Traffic from 3K to 189K Users for an IT Solutions Company

Echo Innovate IT Solutions · 3 Years · SEO Manager

Challenge

The company's website was receiving only 100–150 daily visitors (~3,000–4,500/month) with minimal organic visibility. High-value keywords related to mobile app development and IT outsourcing were dominated by larger competitors, and the site lacked a structured content and backlink strategy.

Strategy & Execution

Developed a full-funnel SEO and content strategy focused on three pillars: technical SEO improvements (site speed, crawlability, schema markup), a content calendar targeting high-intent keywords around mobile app development, and a sustained backlink campaign building high-authority contextual dofollow links. Complemented organic efforts with social media optimisation that increased social traffic by 50%.

Professional Services / CPA Industry

Building a Domain from Scratch to 2,357 Keywords and $2.9K Monthly Traffic Value

Unison Globus Aalliance · 1 Year 5 Months · SEO Manager

Challenge

Unison Globus had a brand-new website with zero organic presence — no indexed pages, no keyword rankings, and no domain authority. The goal was to establish the site as a credible resource in the competitive US-focused CPA and accounting services market.

Strategy & Execution

Built the entire SEO foundation from the ground up: keyword research targeting high-intent CPA queries (e.g. "best CPA near me", "cost to hire a CPA"), created comprehensive pillar content optimised for featured snippets, implemented technical SEO including schema markup and URL structure, and executed a backlink outreach campaign that grew referring domains to 3.1K.
